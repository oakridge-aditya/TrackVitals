About

With it's simple and no frills interface, TrackVitals allows senior citizens to track vital health metrics in a visually appealing format, ensuring ease of use without compromising on functionality. The app's intuitive design helps users effortlessly monitor their weight, temperature, blood pressure, heart rate, oxygen and blood glucose levels, thereby making it an indispensable tool for health management.

Benefits of tracking vital signs

  • Assess your wellbeing: Vitals display a snapshot of what’s going on inside your body. They provide crucial information about your organs, thereby providing medical professionals to assess your wellbeing. Based on the results, a doctor may conduct further tests, diagnose a problem, or suggest lifestyle changes.
  • Prevent misdiagnosis: Vital signs give medical experts a better insight into what the problem is. Tracking your vital signs provides medical professionals with concrete information that they use to assess your health and form a correct diagnosis.
  • Detect underlying health problems: Silent symptoms of diseases cause medical problems to go undetected for years, making them harder to treat. But when you know what your normal vital levels should be, it’s easier to identify a problem.
  • Motivate lifestyle changes: Keeping an eye on your vital signs can be the push you need to establish a healthier life. Vital signs can signal too much caffeine consumption, high stress, and dehydration. After you acknowledge these problems, you can switch to a healthier lifestyle.
